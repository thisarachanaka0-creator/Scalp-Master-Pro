POLICY
Last updated: March 2026 · scalpmaster.online

🔒 We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. We do not sell your data to third parties.

01 · DATA WE COLLECT

We collect only the minimum information required to process your order and provide access to ScalpMaster Pro.

ORDER DATA

TradingView username, email address, selected plan, USDT transaction hash, network used, and timestamp.

USAGE DATA

Anonymous website analytics via Google Analytics — pages visited, time on site, country, device type. No personal identifiers.

WHAT WE DON'T COLLECT

No credit card numbers, no passwords, no government IDs. Crypto payments contain no sensitive financial data.

COOKIES

Google Analytics uses cookies for anonymous tracking. No marketing or tracking cookies are used beyond this.

02 · HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To grant TradingView access to your account after purchase
  • To send order confirmation to your email address
  • To verify your transaction on the blockchain
  • To provide customer support when you contact us
  • To improve our website using anonymous analytics data

03 · DATA SHARING

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third parties.

Your data may be shared only with:

  • TradingView — to grant indicator access to your username (username only)
  • Google Analytics — anonymous website usage data only
  • Telegram — order notification system (internal use only, no third-party access)

04 · DATA RETENTION

We retain your order information (username, email, transaction hash) for as long as your access is active and for a reasonable period afterward for support purposes. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

05 · YOUR RIGHTS

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by using browser Do Not Track settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the Contact page.

06 · BLOCKCHAIN TRANSPARENCY

Please note that cryptocurrency transactions are recorded on a public blockchain. Your wallet address and transaction hash are publicly visible on the blockchain by design — this is outside our control. We do not share your personal details alongside this information.

07 · CONTACT

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, please contact us via the Contact page.
