How do I find my transaction hash? ▾
Go to your exchange (Binance/Bybit etc.) → Wallet → Withdrawal History → find your USDT withdrawal → click it to see the TXID/Hash.
How do I add the indicator on TradingView? ▾
After access is granted: TradingView → Indicators → Invite-only scripts → ScalpMaster Pro → Add to chart.
